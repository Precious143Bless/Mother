A Letter From the Heart

My dearest Mom,

There are words I have carried in my heart for so long, things I feel but don't always say. You gave me your time, your patience, your endless smiles even on tired days. I remember your gentle hands, the warmth of your hugs, and how you always believed in me.

Every sacrifice, every prayer, every little thing you've done, it built a beautiful world for me. I hope today you feel wrapped in the same love you have showered on me all these years.

You are my comfort, and my dearest friend. Thank you for being you, irreplaceable.

Forever grateful,
